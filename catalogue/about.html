折り紙付き

certified patterns from the web

折り紙付き, or origamitsuki: a Japanese phrase meaning guaranteed, authentic, or certified. Here at Origamitsuki all of the patterns are exactly that, with clearly-illustrated steps that show you the way from start to finish. Find the pattern you're looking for with our categories and tags, or be spontaneous and try one of our randomly-selected popular patterns on the first page.

Got a pattern you love that isn't here? Submit your design into our catalogue and share your new folds with the world.
